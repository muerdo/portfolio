The Daily Grind of a Red Teamer: Why Reconnaissance Dominates

OSINT & RECON | 12 MIN READ
Insight: In red team operations, the reconnaissance phase‚Äîencompassing OSINT‚Äîoften accounts for up to 80% of the work. This patience distinguishes junior pentesters from seniors.

Research suggests that over 90% of Fortune 1000 companies rely on Active Directory for managing endpoints and assets, making it a critical target. However, before touching AD, one must understand the external perimeter. This phase involves patient collection of publicly available data from sources like social media, metadata in files, and network details, building a comprehensive attack map.

Essential Toolset

Favorite tools among red teamers include combinations of Google Dorks for advanced searches, Maltego for automating correlation from over 58 data sources like AlienVault OTX and VirusTotal, and Recon-ng for modular web reconnaissance.

Advanced Tactics

Incorporating temporal analysis in OSINT helps predict behaviors, enhancing predictive assessments. Physical recon can merge with OSINT for hybrid paths. Automation scripts like HostEnum.ps1 optimize time-consuming tasks, allowing focus on analysis.

Efficiency and discretion are paramount, as OSINT unlocks targeted attacks by exploiting organizational data without alerting defenses early in the kill chain.
